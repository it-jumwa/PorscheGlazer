Porsche in Motorsport

Porsche is a German sports car manufacturer headquartered in Stuttgart, Baden-Württemberg, Germany. The company is formally known as Dr. Ing h.c.F.Porsche AG, which is short of Ferdinand Porsche's full title in German, Doktor-Ingenieur honoris causa (Doctor of Engineering, Honorary Degree). Ferdinand founded Porsche alongside Adolf Rosenberger and Anton Piëch in 1931.
