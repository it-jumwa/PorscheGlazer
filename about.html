FIA World Endurance Championship

The World Endurance Championship is organized by the Automobile Club de l'Ouest (ACO) and governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). The series features multiple classes, including sports prototypes in the Hypercar class featuring Le Mans Hypercar (LMH) or Le Mans Daytona Hypercar (LMDh), and production-based grand tourers (GT) competing in the LM GT3 class.

WEC: Hypercar

Formula E

The ABB FIA Formula E World Championship is the highest class of competition for electrically powered single-seater racing cars. Races take place internationally, and often on street circuits developed especially for Formula E on closed public roads in major cities.
